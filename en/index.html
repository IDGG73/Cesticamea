About

Cesticamea is a completely FREE trivia game where you can create and share your own questions with the world through the making of Question Packages.

The game offers several pre-made modes for you to play alone, or with company. Up to 4 players on the same device.

Features

  •    From 1 to 4 players.
  •     Create your own Question Packages.
  •     Publish your Question Packages.
  •     A lot of particles in the background. Particle amount can be adjusted.
  •     Satisfying music and sound effects.
  •     Nice and clean UI.

Send a deletion request!

Deletion requests can be sent through Cesticamea, inside the Package Manager.

If you are playing Cesticamea from a different device than the one used to upload that one package, this package is 'technically' not your package anymore. If that's the case, your package won't be displayed inside your list of uploaded packages. If you want me to delete your package, send a message to example@example.com including:

  • Package name.
  • Package upload date.
  • Email attached to the package.

If some of the above data does not apply to your package (or is unknown), you can still send your deletion request, but the chances of being accepted are way lower.
